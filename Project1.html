My role

In this project i was the general UX designer and researcher.
My responsibilities were to:

  • Conduct foundational research for empathizing.
  • Defining problem statement
  • Ideating solutions.
  • Wireframing.
  • Lo-Fi and Hi-Fi prototyping.
  • Conducting usability reaseach and generating insights.
  • Iterating on designs

Project Goal

Design an app for the store that will allow users to remotely search for grocery items, allow users to pickup order from store or have it delivered to their door step at their convienece

Target Audience

Busy workers, commuters and disabled people have trouble finding time for grocery shopping or are unable to travel to the store, also notify customer on special deals ands promotions

Key challenges or constraints

My key challenges were the budget and the short time frame to complete the work, but fortunately I was able to make it.

Research study details

I started the project with a simple survey to find out public opinion on online grocery shopping, the data from the survey allowed me to create personas that helped me to better understand user needs and increase empathy.

Initial design concepts

Taking time to generate these paper wireframes allowed me to organize my thoughts, these simple wireframes provided me a direction to follow when designing digital wirframes, also this method saved time and money on producing slightly more complex digital wireframes which tend to be a more demanding task.
